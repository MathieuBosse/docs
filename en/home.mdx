## Key Features

1. **[Custom Conversational AI](/ai-chatbot)**: Chat, ask questions, or brainstorm with an AI tailored to your business.
2. **[Maximum Security](/security)**: All data remains on your servers, protected and encrypted.
3. **[RAG: Intelligent Document Search](/document-search)**: Query your files (PDF, Word, Excel, code...) and get precise, concise answers.
4. **[Business Fine-Tuning](/fine-tuning)**: Customize the AI's behavior and style according to your specific needs.
5. **[Multimodality](/multimodal-analysis)**: Text, images, and audio analyzed to enrich your interactions.
6. **[Fast Multi-Platform Deployment](/deployment)**: Accessible via browser, mobile, or integrable into your internal tools.

- **Speech synthesis**: Generation of natural and expressive voices
- **Intelligent analysis**: Deep understanding of audio content

## Key features

### Speech recognition
- Real-time transcription with minimal latency
- >95% precision in all environments
- Advanced multilingual support (50+ languages)
- Adaptive learning to your business vocabulary

### Speech synthesis
- Hyper-realistic voices indistinguishable from human voices
- Total personalization for your brand
- Precise emotional control
- Real-time streaming

## Applications

- **Customer service**: Advanced IVR, call transcription, automatic summaries

## 🎬 Three concrete stories

<Tabs>
<Tab title="🏬 A retail chain">
A customer enters the store, looks at a product, asks a question.  
Our AI detects their **interest**, analyzes their voice to identify **hesitation**, and generates in real-time a **personalized offer** displayed on screen.  
👉 Result: +50% conversion.
</Tab>

<Tab title="👩‍⚕️ A medical consultation">
A patient talks about their symptoms. The AI analyzes **words**, but also **voice tone** and **facial expressions**.  
It helps the doctor spot **non-verbal cues** that are crucial.  
👉 Result: more reliable diagnoses and reassured patients.
</Tab>

<Tab title="📞 A support center">
A customer calls while frustrated. Their voice betrays **frustration**.  
The AI automatically routes the call to a consultant trained in emotion management.  
👉 Result: +75% first-contact resolution.
</Tab>
